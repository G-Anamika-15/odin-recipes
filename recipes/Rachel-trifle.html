Rachel's English Trifle!

Why you should be making this dish(beef is the secret)

Ingredients you'll need to recreate this iconic dish

Total time: 30 minutes

  • 1 package lady fingers
  • 1 jar rapberry jam
  • 2 cup custard
  • 1 package raspberries
  • 10 oz ground beef
  • 3/4 cup peas
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 ripe bananas
  • whipped cream

Let's get to making this dish!

  1. Saut√©e the ground beef with peas and onions and prepare a custard ahead of time
  2. Line the bottom of a large clear bowl with a layer of lady fingers.
  3. Add the raspberry jam until the lady fingers are completely coated. Cover the jam layer with half of your custard. Top with raspberries.
  4. Add another layer of lady fingers.
  5. Add a layer of the beef mixture.
  6. Add the rest of your custard and sliced bananas.
  7. Cover with whipped cream and garnish. Serve immediately, before people can run away.
  8. Serve it to your own Joey, Ross and Chandler!
